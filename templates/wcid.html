Homelab Enthusiast & Developer Operations

I host all my services and sites in my beloved server rack with four servers (250+ GB of RAM combined) and local 10-Gig networking! And to brag even more, around 32TB of usable storage are installed in the rack.
Furthermore, I prefer using Docker for everything (like even the most minor thing — for example, this website — is hosted in a container and proxied via Nginx and Caddy). As a continuous integration tool I use a self-hosted Woodpecker CI instance — it’s cool!
